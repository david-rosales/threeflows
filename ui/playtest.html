Welcome!

This station is about responding to students in the moment. It's set in the context of a 7th grade science classroom, in an introductory lesson for photosynthesis.

It's a practice space to learn to apply science inquiry practices, and to use principles from cognitive science to motivate students.

When you're done, we'll also ask you to share some quick feedback.

You have three choices:

  1. If you're an experienced science teacher, we'd love if you could share your feedback on how some undergraduates have responded to these scenarios. Give feedback
  2. If you're an intermediate or beginning science teacher, we'd like you to try out these practice scenarios without any additional scaffolding. Practice
  3. If you have no experience whatsoever in science education, we'd like you to practice some of the scenarios with a little support. With extra support

Thanks!
